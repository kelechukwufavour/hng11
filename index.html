My Tech Goals for the Next 2 years

    Build

    I envision completing the building and launching of my first Web Application.

    Skills Development

    To gain mastery in frontend technologies through daily commitment to increasing knowledge. Major frontend technologies, i have interest in are Javascript Frameworks (React.js, Vue.js and Angular.js)

    Growth and Collaboration

    To continuously contribute to Open Source Communities, gaining hands-on experience, being a team player, and improving versatility and flexibility.

    Empowerment

    To give back to the communities I have benefited from, leaving the door open for those coming behind me. As a woman, building allies is crucial not only for personal growth but also to pave the way for those who follow.

    Networking and Community

    To foster alliances by networking and being part of Tech Communities. A revelation during the recent She Code Africa summit left a lasting impression on me: "Behind a successful woman is a whole village of helpers" (Jola Aderemi-Makinde, 2023). This insight highlighted the significance of teamwork in achieving success, emphasizing the importance of leaning on others for support. Reflecting on this, I embraced the notion that, like a garden composed of diverse flowers, a community thrives on a variety of strengths, perspectives, and characteristics.

    Earn From My Skill

    Having gained enough knowledge and mastery in frontend technologies, I aim to land a well-paying job role, thereby putting my skills to use.
